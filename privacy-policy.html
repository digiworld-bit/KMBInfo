Privacy Policy – KMBInfo

KMBInfo is developed to help parents, guardians, and school staff access school-related information and updates. We respect user privacy and are committed to protecting it.

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Data is used strictly for school communication purposes.

Children’s Privacy

KMBInfo is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. The app is designed for parents, guardians, and authorized school staff. Children should not create accounts or use the app independently.
